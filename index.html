About AZITCC

Advanced Zone of Information Technology Certification Center (AZITCC) is a leading educational institution in Kabul, Afghanistan, dedicated to providing world-class IT education and professional certifications.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to bridging the gap between traditional education and industry demands, preparing our students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technology sector.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality IT education that empowers individuals and strengthens communities.

Our Vision

To be the premier technology education center in Afghanistan, recognized for excellence and innovation.
